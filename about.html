About us

New Company for Trading & Contracting (NCTC) is a Lebanese contracting firm specializing in building construction, infrastructure, and public works. Since its establishment, the company has held a leading position in the Lebanese construction sector, known for its commitment to quality, integrity, and excellence. Founded in 1985, the company was built on a vision of high ambition and professional excellence. From the very beginning, it has been entrusted with and has successfully executed some of the most prestigious and high-profile projects in Lebanon, a testament to its expertise, reliability, and strong reputation in the industry.

Our team

At NCTC, we believe that the strength of any successful project lies in its team. That’s why we’ve brought together a group of highly skilled engineers, supervisors, and technicians with extensive experience in general contracting and construction project execution. Our team includes experts in civil and architectural engineering, project management professionals, and well-trained technical staff who are committed to the highest standards of quality and safety. We work as one unit to ensure every project is delivered efficiently, on time, and tailored precisely to client requirements. From planning to handover, our focus is on precision, innovation, and attention to detail. We leverage the latest engineering technologies and best practices to provide sustainable, effective solutions that meet market demands and exceed client expectations.
